Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients: Paneer cubes, tomato puree, onion chopped, ginger-garlic paste, cream, garam masala, red chili powder, sugar, salt, butter, cook onion and tomato base, add spices and cream, mix paneer, simmer.

  1. Heat butter, sauté onions, add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomato puree, spices, and cook until thick.
  3. Add cream and paneer cubes. Simmer for 5 mins.
  4. Serve with naan or paratha.

Rajma (Kidney Bean Curry)

Ingredients: Kidney beans soaked overnight, onions chopped, tomatoes pureed, ginger-garlic paste, cumin seeds, coriander powder, garam masala, salt, oil, fresh coriander, pressure cook rajma, cook onion-tomato masala, add rajma, simmer, garnish.

  1. Pressure cook rajma with salt until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil, add cumin seeds.
  3. Add onions, sauté, then add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree.
  4. Add spices and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add rajma with water. Simmer for 15 mins.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice.

Masala Dosa with Potato Filling

Ingredients: Boiled potatoes mashed, onion sliced, mustard seeds, green chili chopped, turmeric, curry leaves, salt, oil, dosa batter (rice, urad dal soaked and fermented), prepare potato masala, spread dosa, add filling, fold and serve.

  1. Prepare dosa batter by soaking, grinding, and fermenting.
  2. For filling: heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, chili, turmeric, and mashed potatoes. Cook well.
  3. Spread dosa on hot tawa, cook until golden.
  4. Add filling and fold. Serve with chutney and sambar.

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Ingredients: Chickpeas soaked overnight, onions chopped, tomatoes pureed, ginger-garlic paste, chole masala, cumin seeds, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, oil, fresh coriander, pressure cook chickpeas, sauté onions and spices, add puree, mix chickpeas, simmer, garnish.

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt until soft.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds.
  3. Add onions and sauté till golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, and spices. Cook till oil separates.
  5. Add chickpeas and some water. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve with bhature or rice.

Gulab Jamun (Sweet Dessert)

Ingredients: Milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, ghee, milk (to knead), oil/ghee for frying, sugar, water, cardamom pods, saffron strands, make dough, fry balls, prepare syrup, soak and serve warm.

  1. Make syrup by boiling sugar, water, cardamom, and saffron. Set aside.
  2. Make dough using milk powder, flour, soda, ghee, and milk.
  3. Roll into smooth balls.
  4. Fry in medium-hot oil until golden.
  5. Soak in warm syrup for at least 1 hour before serving.

Suji Halwa (Semolina Pudding):

Ingredients: Suji (semolina), ghee, sugar, water, cardamom powder, chopped cashews, almonds, raisins, heat ghee, roast suji till golden, boil water and sugar separately, add to roasted suji, stir continuously, add cardamom and dry fruits, cook till thick and aromatic.

  1. Heat ghee in a heavy pan, add suji (semolina) to it.
  2. Roast the suji on medium flame, keep stirring until it turns golden brown and gives a nice aroma.
  3. In a separate pan, boil water and sugar together until sugar dissolves completely
  4. Slowly pour the hot sugar water into the roasted suji mixture while stirring continuously
  5. Mix well, keep stirring to avoid lumps, and cook on low flame until the mixture thickens and ghee starts separating from the sides.
  6. Add chopped dry fruits like cashews, almonds, and raisins. Stir everything for another 1–2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot as a dessert or during festivals like Navratri, Diwali, or as prasad.
